# VoiceToys develops and changes thanks to you.

From its beginning, VoiceToys has developed, grown, changed and advanced through communication with those who use it.

We realized that its possibilities and ways of application are practically unlimited, and that is why we conceived it as a platform that can be improved and enriched with new functionalities according to the wishes and ideas of its users.

We are proud that together with you we are creating an innovation that represents the creativity of local engineers and therapists.

That is why we invite you to contact us with your ideas and take part in the further development of VoiceToys!
